    Game Features

    Experience unrestriced movement like never before with unshackled traversal.

    You can navigate through the world with ease by swimming, climbing, vaulting, jumping off ledges, or hoisting yourself up them while using a platformer-based navigation system that deviates from the traditional tileset-bound mobility.

    Set out on a voyage that is engaging and filled with engrossing stories.

    Get ready to be mesmerized by a plethora of unique characters and compelling narratives that will carry you away on a captivating voyage. As Sea of Ftars performs its RPG duty by delving into time-honored themes of adventure and the ties of friendship, you'll encounter moments of epic grandeur, whimsical fun, and profound emotions.
